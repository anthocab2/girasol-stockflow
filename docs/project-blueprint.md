# Girasol StockFlow - Project Blueprint

## Overview

Girasol StockFlow is a digital inventory and sales tracking system designed for Hotel Girasol operations. The goal is to help employees register product sales faster, reduce inventory errors, and provide administrators with a clear history of product movement.

## Problem

The current inventory process for snacks, beverages, alcohol, and other products can take around 30 to 45 minutes. Since sales and product movements may be tracked manually, it can be difficult to identify where inventory discrepancies happen.

## Solution

The system allows employees to log in using their punch number and register sales from a mobile-friendly interface. Each sale includes the product, quantity, room number, payment method, employee, date, and time. Inventory is updated automatically after every sale.

Administrators can review all sales records, employee activity, inventory levels, and product movements.

## Target Users

### Employee
- Logs in using a punch number
- Registers product sales
- Selects room number and payment method
- Views personal sales activity

### Administrator
- Manages products
- Reviews full sales history
- Tracks inventory levels
- Reviews employee activity
- Identifies possible inventory discrepancies
